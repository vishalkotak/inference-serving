Your README must answer these

This is the bar for the core sprint (Weeks 1–2). Hit all ten clearly and you have a strong first inference artifact; Weeks 3–8 build depth on top.

  1. What model did you serve?
  2. What hardware did you use?
  3. What is your baseline throughput?
  4. What is your p50 / p99 latency?
  5. How does concurrency change throughput?
  6. Where is the latency / throughput knee?
  7. How much memory does the KV cache consume — and is decode compute- or memory-bound on your GPU (roofline)?
  8. What changed with prefix caching or quantization — and did quality hold (evals)?
  9. What metrics would you alert on in production?
  10. What would you do next?
